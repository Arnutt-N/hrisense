# Product

## Register

product

## Users

HR managers and analysts working in government organizations. They use this dashboard in professional settings to monitor organizational health, identify personnel risks before they escalate, and make data-driven workforce decisions. The context is analytical and strategic—they need clarity, not decoration.

## Product Purpose

HRiSENSE is a proactive workforce analytics system that transforms raw personnel data into actionable intelligence. It exists to:

- **Identify risks early**: Surface retirement waves, vacancy gaps, and high-risk personnel situations before they become crises
- **Enable strategic planning**: Provide the data foundation for long-term workforce capacity and succession planning
- **Support operational tracking**: Keep personnel status, alerts, and organizational metrics visible and current
- **Guide decisions**: Turn analytics into concrete recommendations for HR actions and resource allocation

Success means HR teams can anticipate problems months in advance, plan with confidence, and act on evidence rather than intuition.

## Brand Personality

**Approachable, helpful, organized**

The voice is clear and supportive—like a knowledgeable colleague who makes complex data feel manageable. Not cold or robotic, not overly casual. The interface guides users through dense information with calm confidence, making the complexity of workforce analytics feel navigable.

## Anti-references

- **Not a generic SaaS dashboard**: Avoid template-y admin panels with cold blue gradients, stock chart styling, and cookie-cutter layouts
- **Not bureaucratic government software**: Avoid dense, poorly-organized tables with cramped spacing and outdated visual language
- **Not consumer-app casual**: This handles sensitive personnel data—avoid playful patterns, excessive animation, or decorative flourishes that undermine professionalism

The balance: professional and trustworthy without being stiff, modern and clear without being casual.

## Design Principles

1. **Clarity over cleverness**: Data and insights should be immediately readable. No decorative elements that compete with the information.

2. **Proactive, not reactive**: Surface what matters before the user has to hunt for it. Risk indicators, upcoming retirements, and alerts should be visible and contextual.

3. **Organized complexity**: Workforce data is inherently complex. The interface should feel structured and hierarchical, helping users navigate from overview to detail without confusion.

4. **Approachable professionalism**: Make the system feel like a helpful expert, not a cold database. Clear language, thoughtful spacing, and intentional visual hierarchy.

5. **Thai-first design**: This is a Thai-language system. Typography, spacing, and layout should serve Thai text naturally—not force English design patterns onto Thai content.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- **WCAG 2.1 AA compliance**: Meet standard government/enterprise accessibility requirements
- **Thai language accessibility**: Proper rendering of Thai text with appropriate line heights, font sizes, and line-breaking behavior. Use Noto Sans Thai for consistent, readable Thai typography.
- **Data visualization accessibility**: Charts, risk indicators, and KPI cards need clear labels, sufficient color contrast, and text alternatives. Risk status (green/amber/red) should not rely on color alone.
- **Keyboard navigation**: All interactive elements (tables, filters, navigation) must be fully keyboard-accessible
- **Screen reader support**: Proper ARIA labels for charts, data tables, and dynamic content
